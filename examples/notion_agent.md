# Competitive Brief — Notion

## Overview
Notion is a productivity and note-taking application developed by Notion Labs, Inc. It serves as a workspace for notetaking, knowledge management, data organization, and project and task tracking.

## Products & Services
- Notion
- Notion Agents
- Notion Templates

## Market Positioning
Notion is an all-in-one workspace that combines note-taking, project management, and task organization. It allows users to create customized databases, documents, and calendars to streamline their personal and professional workflows.

## Recent News
- Notion 3.2: Mobile AI, new models, people directory
- External Agents API
- Partnership with Claude, Codex, Decagon

## Background & Role
Narendra Modi is the Prime Minister of India, born on September 17, 1950, in Vadnagar, Gujarat, to a Gujarati Hindu family, and is affiliated with the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP). He served as the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014 and has been Prime Minister since 2014.

## Policy Positions

- Introduced the Citizenship Amendment Act, which sparked controversy, according to Wikipedia
- Credited for encouraging economic growth in Gujarat during his tenure as Chief Minister, according to Wikipedia and Britannica
- Supported the restoration of ancient sites, such as the Kashi Vishwanath Temple, according to Economic Times

# Competitive Brief — Notion

## Overview
Notion is a productivity and note-taking application that serves as a workspace for notetaking, knowledge management, data organization, and project and task tracking.

## Products & Services
- Notion AI
- Notion Calendar
- Notion Mail

## Market Positioning
Notion aims to break away from traditional tools and bring back ideas from early computing pioneers, such as amplifying imagination and augmenting intellect, by providing a flexible and customizable all-in-one workspace.

## Recent News
- Notion has crossed $500 million in annual revenue and launched a customizable AI agent.
- Notion has seen a significant increase in adoption of its AI features, with 50% of customers now paying for AI add-ons.
- Notion was valued at around $11 billion in January 2026, with $600 million in revenue.

## ⚠️ Caveats & Gaps
There are discrepancies in the reported number of users, with some sources stating over 20 million users and others stating over 100 million users. Additionally, the sources may not reflect the most up-to-date information, as the latest available data is from 2026.
